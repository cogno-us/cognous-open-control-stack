# Open-Source Boundary

The public layer includes:
- hub documentation;
- component repo links;
- formats;
- schemas;
- validators;
- reference behavior;
- examples;
- tests;
- business-facing documentation.

The commercial or product layer may include:
- hosted services;
- integrations;
- enterprise storage;
- identity integration;
- approval workflows;
- compliance mappings;
- replay viewers;
- evidence registries;
- dashboards;
- managed policy workflows;
- customer-specific deployment support;
- SLAs and support.

Open the record formats. Compete on implementation, governance, and operations.
